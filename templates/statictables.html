VC-Tune: introduction

Automatic Suggestions

Exhausted when picking a suitable task fragmentation with VC- Systems? VC-Tune is here to help you. VC-Tune enables easy exploration of different task fragmentations schemes in a simple, online, and interactive manner. VC-Tune can also give you a direct suggestion of a suitable task fragmentation scheme.

Plenty of Interaction

A user can fully interact with VC-Tune to get an intuition of the best task fragmentation schemes, depending on various user specified settings.

Easy Extension

VC-Tune is a prototype system, but it can be very simple to extend to other VC-systems, datasets or real-world tasks, due to its general design.
